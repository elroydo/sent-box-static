About Us
We, Sent-Box, are a retailer that specialize in selling various fragrances from prominent brands such as Hugo Boss, Calvin Klein, Lacoste and many more. We target our products towards both sexes from ages of 16-30 year olds.
Becuase we are a smallbusiness, we've decided to have an online presence in order to gain attraction and appeal to a wider audience from anywhere around the world. The purpose of this website is for it to be a marketing medium to promote our products and advertise present offers available to our customers.
Be sure to pay us a visit in-store for special offers and discounts!
